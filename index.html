About

A Life in Service of
Sacred Knowledge

"The title Sidhanti is not merely a name — it is a recognition of mastery in the classical Siddhanta texts of Vedic astrology, earned through decades of rigorous study and practice."

Sidhanti Sri Sivaprasad has dedicated over four decades to the sacred sciences of Jyotishya and Vastu Shastra. Rooted in the South Indian Siddhanta tradition — using the classical square chart and the Nirayana sidereal system — his readings carry the weight of lineage-based learning.

His expertise in Vastu Shastra is particularly renowned, having transformed hundreds of homes, offices and commercial establishments. For NRI families, he offers full video consultations — authentic South Indian guidance, wherever you are in the world.
