What is Vastu Shastra?

వాస్తు శాస్త్రం అంటే ఏమిటి?

✦

Vastu Shastra is the ancient Indian science of architecture and spatial arrangement that harmonizes a building with the five elements (Pancha Bhoota) — earth, water, fire, air and space. When a home or workplace is aligned with these natural forces and the eight directions, it becomes a source of health, happiness and prosperity.

Sidhanti Sri Sivaprasad's expertise in Vastu Shastra spans over 40 years, encompassing hundreds of residential homes, offices, commercial establishments, factories and industrial spaces across Andhra Pradesh and neighbouring states.

Why Vastu Matters

  • 🏠 Health & Harmony: Correct directional placement reduces stress, improves relationships and promotes physical wellbeing.
  • 💰 Wealth & Prosperity: Vastu-aligned spaces attract financial growth and protect against unnecessary losses.
  • 🏢 Business Success: Offices and commercial spaces aligned with Vastu principles see improved productivity and growth.
  • 🔄 Corrections Without Demolition: Most Vastu doshas can be corrected with adjustments, not full rebuilding.

What a Vastu Consultation Covers

✦

🏠 Residential Checklist

  • Main entrance direction and placement
  • Master bedroom, children's room alignment
  • Kitchen direction and stove placement
  • Pooja room / prayer space location

  • Windows, ventilation and light flow

  • Recommendations for remedies and corrections

🏢 Commercial Checklist

  • Owner / MD cabin placement

  • Cash counter and safe location
  • Accounts and HR seating
  • Pantry / cafeteria direction
  • Conference and meeting room
  • Server room / machinery placement
  • Branding and signage direction
  • Remedies without major structural changes
