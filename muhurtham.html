The Importance of Muhurtham

✦

Every important event in life — a marriage, a new home, a business, a journey — is best begun at the right moment. Muhurtham is the science of selecting that perfect window of time when planetary energies align to support your endeavour and protect it from obstacles.

💒

Vivaha Muhurtham

వివాహ ముహూర్తం

Marriage date and time selection considering both horoscopes, Lagna, Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga and avoidance of inauspicious periods (Rahu Kaal, Yama Gandam).

🏠

Griha Pravesham

గృహ ప్రవేశం ముహూర్తం

Housewarming ceremony muhurtham — auspicious date and time for entering a new home, ensuring health, happiness and prosperity for the family.

🏪

Business / Shop Opening

వ్యాపార శుభారంభం

Auspicious muhurtham for launching a new business, shop opening, company registration or office inauguration for lasting success.

👶

Namakaran (Naming Ceremony)

నామకరణం

Muhurtham for baby naming ceremony and selection of a Nakshatra-appropriate first letter for the child's name.

✈️

Travel & Journey Muhurtham

Auspicious departure time for long journeys, travel abroad, joining a new job in a new city, or relocating for education.

📅

Annual Muhurtham Calendar

A personalized calendar of all auspicious dates for the coming year — for weddings, Griha Pravesham, business events, and important personal milestones.

How Muhurtham is Calculated

✦

🌙 Factors Considered

  • Tithi (lunar day) — must be auspicious
  • Vara (day of week) — appropriate for the event
  • Nakshatra (star of the day) — auspicious stars preferred
  • Yoga (sun-moon combination) — Siddha, Amrita yogas preferred
  • Karana (half-day unit) — must be favourable
  • Lagna (rising sign at the selected time)
  • Avoidance of Rahu Kaal, Yama Gandam, Gulika Kaal
  • Personal horoscopes of involved parties

📋 What to Share with Us

  • Type of event (marriage, housewarming, etc.)
  • Approximate date range you're considering
  • Horoscopes of all key persons involved
  • City/location where the event will take place
  • Any constraints (venue bookings, availability)
  • Multiple muhurtham options provided where possible
  • Explanation of selection given in Telugu or English
