A Life Devoted to Sacred Knowledge

✦

Sidhanti Sri Sivaprasad has dedicated more than four decades of his life to the sacred sciences of Jyotishya (Vedic Astrology) and Vastu Shastra. Born and rooted in Andhra Pradesh, he carries forward the classical South Indian tradition of astrology — the Siddhanta school — which uses the distinctive square chart (South Indian style) and the sidereal Nirayana system, following the lineage of the great sage Parashara.

The title "Sidhanti" (సిద్ధాంతి) is an honoured designation in South Indian tradition, given to those who are proficient in the Siddhanta — the authoritative texts and calculations of Vedic astrology. It is a mark of deep learning, not merely practice.

Over the decades, Sidhanti Sri Sivaprasad has guided thousands of families across Andhra Pradesh in matters of marriage, career, health, home construction, business decisions, and spiritual growth. His approach is grounded, compassionate, and deeply rooted in classical principles — offering clarity without creating unnecessary fear.

His expertise in Vastu Shastra is particularly well-regarded, having transformed hundreds of residences, offices, commercial establishments and industrial spaces. He is sought after for both new construction Vastu planning and for correcting existing spaces — often without requiring structural changes.
