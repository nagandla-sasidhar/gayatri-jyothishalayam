What We Analyse

✦

🔢 The 10 Porutham (South Indian)

  • Dina Porutham — health and longevity
  • Gana Porutham — nature and temperament
  • Mahendra Porutham — prosperity and children
  • Stree Deergha Porutham — wife's long life
  • Yoni Porutham — physical compatibility
  • Rasi Porutham — emotional harmony
  • Rajju Porutham — husband's longevity (critical)
  • Vedha Porutham — obstacles and ill effects
  • Vasya Porutham — mutual attraction
  • Nadi Porutham — health of offspring (critical)

📋 What to Bring / Share

  • Full name, date of birth, time of birth
  • Place of birth (city/village)
  • Existing horoscope document (if available)
  • Both bride's and groom's details required
  • In-person or share via WhatsApp before call
  • Results explained clearly in Telugu or English
  • Written summary provided after consultation
  • Follow-up queries answered
