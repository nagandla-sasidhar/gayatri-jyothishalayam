South Indian Jyotishya

సిద్ధాంత జ్యోతిష్యం

✦

South Indian Vedic Astrology (Siddhanta Jyotishya) uses the distinctive square chart format and follows the Nirayana (sidereal) system — the same tradition followed by the great sage Parashara. Sidhanti Sri Sivaprasad is a trained practitioner of this classical tradition, with 40+ years of reading thousands of charts.

Unlike Western astrology, Vedic astrology places primary importance on the Moon sign (Chandra Rashi), Nakshatra (birth star) and the Dasha (planetary period) system — providing remarkably accurate time-specific predictions for life events.

Key Differences: South Indian Chart

  • ✦Fixed house positions — the 12 houses are fixed, and the signs move (opposite of North Indian style)
  • ✦Nakshatra emphasis — the 27 birth stars form the foundation of prediction and Dasha calculation
  • ✦Vimshottari Dasha — 120-year planetary period cycle used for accurate timing of life events
  • ✦Ashtakavarga — point-based strength system unique to South Indian analysis
