## Phase 2 — Operations (Weeks 4–8)

### 2.1 Online Booking
- **Calendly** (free plan) for phone/video consultations
- Booking types: "30-min Phone Consult", "60-min Full Kundli", "Vastu Site Visit Enquiry"
- Add Calendly link to website contact page and WhatsApp bio

### 2.2 Online Payments
- **Razorpay** or **Instamoop** (free setup, ~2% per transaction)
- Create service-specific payment links
- UPI QR code for in-person visits

### 2.3 Client Database (Google Sheets to start)
Track: Name · Phone · DOB · Service · Date · City · Language · Follow-up Due

Key insight by service:
- **Muhurtham clients** → note their upcoming life events, reach out proactively next year
- **Match Making clients** → strong referral network (siblings, cousins, friends)
- **Vastu clients** → commercial clients refer other businesses

### 2.4 Enable NRI Video Consultations
- Use Google Meet or Zoom
- For Vastu: offer video walkthrough (client shares phone camera of home)
- NRI clients (Gulf, US, UK, Singapore, Australia) pay 3–5× local rates
- This unlocks a large, underserved market seeking authentic South Indian astrologers

## Phase 3 — Content & Social Proof (Months 2–4)

### 3.1 YouTube Channel (Highest long-term ROI)
Post in Telugu + optional English subtitles. Target diaspora.

Content ideas:
- "What is South Indian Kundli? How is it different?"
- "Top 5 Vastu mistakes people make in a new home"
- "How to choose a marriage muhurtham"
- "Numerology: how your name affects your life"
- "Vastu for flats vs independent houses"
- Monthly rashi predictions
- Q&A: "You asked, he answers" series

> One strong Vastu video can reach 10,000–1,00,000 views organically and convert directly to paid consultations.

### 3.2 Instagram / Facebook Page
- Post 3–4×/week using Canva templates (free)
- Content: daily rashi tips, Vastu tip Reels, muhurtham calendar posts, client success stories
- Post in Telugu with English translation — reaches both local and diaspora

### 3.3 Testimonials Strategy
- After every consultation: "Would you share a few words about your experience?"
- Screenshot WhatsApp messages → post on Instagram + add to website
- **Video testimonials** (30 seconds on client's phone) are extremely powerful for Vastu and Match Making

## Phase 5 — Network & Brand Building (Months 4–12)

### 5.1 Wedding Industry Network (Match Making + Muhurtham)
Build cross-referral relationships with:
- Wedding planners and decorators
- Marriage hall / mandapam owners
- Wedding photographers
- Caterers

These people interact with couples preparing for weddings — perfect referral pipeline.

### 5.2 Real Estate / Builder Partnerships (Vastu)
- Approach local builders: "Offer complimentary Vastu consultation to homebuyers as a value-add"
- Builder gets a selling point; you get a steady stream of paying clients
- One builder relationship = 20–50 Vastu clients/year
- Target: new restaurants, clinics, offices, factories opening in the area

### 5.3 NRI Community Outreach
- South Indian community associations abroad (Telugu Association, Tamil Sangam) regularly invite astrologers
- Offer a free webinar for their community → converts to paid clients
- NRI WhatsApp groups for South Indians — get family members to share content

### 5.4 Formalize Referral Program
- "Refer a friend → get your next consultation at 20% discount"
- Match Making clients have the best referral network

### 5.5 Media & Festival Visibility
- Regional Telugu newspapers/TV channels feature astrologers during: Ugadi, Navratri, Pongal, wedding season, New Year
- Reach out to cultural editors of regional newspapers
- Community radio (Telugu FM channels in cities)
